### Step 2: Prepare Incorporation Documents
- **Form 2:** Application for Registration of a Company
- **Constitution / Articles of Association:** Standard template available from IPA
- **Required Details:**
  - Full legal name of company: Vanguard Elite Services Limited
  - Registered office address (must be a physical address in PNG)
  - Postal address
  - Director details: Roger Kumin (sole director)
  - Shareholder details: Roger Kumin (100% shareholder)
  - Nature of business: Security Services (Class A -- Unarmed Guarding)
  - Share structure (e.g., 100 ordinary shares at K1 each)

### Step 3: Lodge Incorporation Application
- **Fee:** ~K500 (company registration)
- **Submit:** All completed forms + constitution to Registrar of Companies
- **Required attachments:**
  - Completed Form 2
  - Company constitution
  - Consent of director(s) to act
  - Consent of shareholder(s)
  - Name reservation confirmation
- **Processing Time:** 5-10 business days typically

### Step 4: Receive Certificate of Incorporation
- **Outcome:** Certificate of Incorporation issued by Registrar of Companies
- **This document is required for:** SIA license application, IRC registration, bank account opening
