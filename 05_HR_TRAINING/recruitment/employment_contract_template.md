## 8. CODE OF CONDUCT

8.1 The Employee agrees to:
   - Comply with all SOPs, site instructions, and lawful directives
   - Maintain professional conduct and appearance at all times
   - Not consume alcohol or drugs before or during shifts
   - Not sleep while on duty
   - Not engage in theft, fraud, bribery, or corruption
   - Not carry unauthorized weapons
   - Maintain confidentiality of all client and company information
   - Treat all persons with dignity and respect
   - Report any incidents promptly and accurately

8.2 Breach of the Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action including warnings, suspension, or dismissal.

---

## 9. TERMINATION

9.1 **During probation:** Either party may terminate with [7] days' written notice.
9.2 **After probation:** Either party may terminate with [14] days' written notice, or payment in lieu of notice.
9.3 **Summary dismissal:** The Employer may dismiss the Employee without notice for:
   - Gross misconduct (theft, violence, intoxication on duty, fraud)
   - Repeated breach of SOPs or Code of Conduct after written warnings
   - Abandonment of post (failure to report for [3] consecutive shifts without notice)
   - Criminal conviction relevant to security duties
9.4 Upon termination, the Employee must return all company property including uniform, equipment, ID card, and keys.
