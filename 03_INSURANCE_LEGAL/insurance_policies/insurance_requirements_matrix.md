## Client Contract Insurance Requirements

Most clients (especially forestry/extractive) will require:
- Certificate of Currency for Public Liability
- Proof of Workers' Compensation coverage
- Named as "Interested Party" on your policy (some clients)
- Minimum cover levels specified in contract (usually K1M+ public liability)

**Action:** When formalizing forestry contract, confirm client's insurance requirements early.
