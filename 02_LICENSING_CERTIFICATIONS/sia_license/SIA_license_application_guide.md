## Post-License Requirements

- Display license at registered office (legal requirement)
- All guards must carry valid permits while on duty
- License valid for 1 year from date of issue
- Renewal requires: current IPA compliance certificate, IRC TIN, insurance certificates (workers comp + public liability)
- Maintain accurate guard register

---

## Important Legal Notes

- **Section 50 violation:** K1,000 fine for operating without a license
- **Section 51 violation:** K1,000 fine for employing guards without permits
- SIA does NOT issue firearms licenses -- that is Police Commissioner's authority
- SIA does NOT enforce minimum wage -- labor disputes go to Department of Labour
