# Vanguard Elite Services -- PNG Regulatory Compliance Checklist

**Last Updated:** 2026-01-30

---

## 1. Business Registration (IPA -- Investment Promotion Authority)

- [ ] Decide legal entity type: **Sole Proprietorship** vs. **Limited Company**
  - *Recommendation:* Register as a limited company (Vanguard Elite Services Limited) for liability protection and client credibility, even as a solo founder
- [ ] Reserve company name with IPA (Form 1 -- Name Reservation)
- [ ] Prepare and lodge incorporation documents:
  - [ ] Form 2 -- Application for Registration
  - [ ] Constitution / Articles of Association
  - [ ] Details of directors, shareholders, registered office
- [ ] Obtain Certificate of Incorporation
- [ ] Register business name (if trading name differs from legal name)
- [ ] Obtain IPA Compliance Certificate (annual requirement)

**Estimated IPA Costs:**
- Name reservation: ~K100
- Company registration: ~K500-K1,000
- Annual compliance: ~K250

---

## 2. Tax Registration (IRC -- Internal Revenue Commission)

- [ ] Register for TIN (Taxpayer Identification Number)
- [ ] Register for GST (if turnover expected to exceed K250,000/year)
- [ ] Register for Salary & Wages Tax (employer obligations)
- [ ] Understand and set up Superannuation contributions (Nasfund/Nambawan Super)

**Notes:**
- TIN registration is free
- GST registration may not be immediately required if initial revenue is below threshold
- Salary & Wages Tax obligations begin when you hire staff

---

## 3. Security Industries Authority (SIA) Licensing

- [ ] Determine license class(es) to apply for:
  - [ ] Class A -- Unarmed Guarding Services (RECOMMENDED FIRST)
  - [ ] Class B -- Armed Guarding Services (Phase 2)
  - [ ] Class C -- Security Consulting/Technology (Phase 2)
- [ ] Complete SIA Application Form (Form 3 for Class A/B, Form 4 for Class C)
- [ ] Prepare supporting documents:
  - [ ] Certificate of Incorporation
  - [ ] Police clearance for founder/directors
  - [ ] Evidence of financial capacity
  - [ ] Proposed operational plan
  - [ ] Details of management and key personnel
  - [ ] Training program outline for guards
- [ ] Pay SIA license fees (refer to License-Permit-Fee-Schedule.pdf)
- [ ] Submit application and attend any required interviews
- [ ] Obtain SIA License Certificate
- [ ] Display license at registered office (legal requirement)

**Key SIA Requirements:**
- Must have a registered business entity before applying
- Background checks on all directors/owners
- Demonstrated financial viability
- Approved training program for security personnel
- License renewal: Annual

---

## 4. Firearms Licensing (Class B Only -- Phase 2)

- [ ] Apply to Registrar of Firearms for company firearms license
- [ ] Establish compliant armory/firearms storage facility:
  - [ ] Steel cabinet/safe bolted to structure
  - [ ] Separate ammunition storage
  - [ ] Access log system
  - [ ] Location approved by police
- [ ] Individual firearms licenses for each armed guard
- [ ] Firearms training certification for all armed personnel
- [ ] Ammunition register and tracking system

**Note:** Refer to "The Registrar of Firearms (draft).docx" in your files

---

## 5. Insurance Requirements

- [ ] Public Liability Insurance (MANDATORY -- minimum K1M cover recommended)
- [ ] Workers Compensation Insurance (MANDATORY under PNG law)
- [ ] Professional Indemnity Insurance (recommended for Class C)
- [ ] Employer's Liability Insurance
- [ ] Motor Vehicle Insurance (if patrol vehicles used)
- [ ] Fidelity/Employee Dishonesty Insurance (recommended)
- [ ] Firearms/Armed Response Insurance (Class B -- significantly higher premiums)

**Insurance Brokers in PNG:**
- Pacific MMI Insurance
- QBE Insurance (PNG)
- Capital Insurance Group
- Marsh PNG

- [ ] Set up Salary & Wages Tax withholding
- [ ] Register with superannuation fund (Nasfund or Nambawan Super)
- [ ] Prepare workplace Health & Safety policy
- [ ] Understand leave entitlements (annual, sick, public holidays)
